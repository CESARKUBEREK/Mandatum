Regulatory authority
ChileCompra
Dirección ChileCompra, under the Ministerio de Hacienda. Governs all public procurement above threshold values, including frameworks, concessions, and direct contracting.
chilecompra.cl
Portal — Mercado Público
Licitaciones Públicas (LP) — open competitive tenders
Trato Directo (TD) — direct contracting with justification
Convenio Marco — framework agreements via catalogue
Grandes Compras — high-value competitive processes

SHCP oversees public procurement governed by LAASSP (goods and services) and LOPSRM (public works). Compliance with SFP (Secretaría de la Función Pública) oversight is mandatory.

Colombia
SECOP · Colombia Compra Eficiente · Estatuto General de Contratación
Live
Regulatory authority
Colombia Compra Eficiente
Agencia Nacional de Contratación Pública. Governs procurement under Ley 80 de 1993 and Ley 1150 de 2007. SECOP II is the mandatory electronic platform for all public entities since 2020.
colombiacompra.gov.co
Portal — SECOP I & II
Licitación pública — open competitive bidding
Selección abreviada — simplified competitive process
Concurso de méritos — merit-based selection
Acuerdos Marco de Precio — CCE framework agreements
community.secop.gov.co

SARLAFT and OFAC sanctions screening
RUP (Registro Único de Proponentes) compliance flagging

Argentina
COMPR.AR · Oficina Nacional de Contrataciones · Decreto 1023/01
Live
Regulatory authority
Oficina Nacional de Contrataciones
ONC under the Jefatura de Gabinete de Ministros. Governs federal procurement under Decreto 1023/2001 and Decreto 1030/2016 (RCAN). COMPR.AR is the unified digital procurement platform since 2021.
argentina.gob.ar/onc

Brasil
Compras.gov.br · Lei 14.133/2021 (Nova Lei de Licitações) · Portuguese language
Live · PT
Regulatory authority
Governo Federal do Brasil
Ministério da Gestão e da Inovação em Serviços Públicos. Procurement governed by the Nova Lei de Licitações (Lei 14.133/2021), which replaced Lei 8.666/93. Full transition mandated since December 2023.
