The core insight
The problem that creates the market
Companies don't lose bids because they lack effort. They lose because they make the wrong decisions at the wrong time — on instinct, in spreadsheets, by whoever has the most seniority in the room.
Mandatum replaces personality-driven pursuit decisions with fast, structured, data-backed executive mandates — across the full lifecycle from first tender to final signature. The platform doesn't replace the bid team. It gives them a decision engine they can defend to their CFO, their legal counsel, and their board.
