Common questions
Can I start pay-as-you-go and move to a subscription?
Yes. Pay-as-you-go has no commitment. Once you process 4–5 gates per month, the subscription pays for itself. We'll show you the breakeven calculation before you commit to anything.
Does Gate II or III require uploading confidential data to your servers?
No. Gates II and III run inside an isolated tenant — your dedicated Mandatum Cloud, your private cloud, or your on-premise environment. Your pricing and contract data never reach our infrastructure.
What happens to our data if we cancel?
You export everything in open formats before cancellation. We execute certified crypto-shredding of all copies within 30 days. Your Decision History Audit remains yours — we deliver it in full on exit.
Is there a minimum contract length for Governance or Sovereign?
Governance is available monthly or annually (20% savings). Sovereign typically runs on 12–36 month contracts given deployment complexity, but all terms are negotiated per engagement.
